🧠 APPLICATIONS OF DBMS (WITH INTERVIEW QUESTIONS)

------------------------------------------------------------
📌 1. INTRODUCTION
------------------------------------------------------------

DBMS is widely used in real-world systems where large amounts of data need to be stored, managed, and retrieved efficiently.

👉 Almost every modern application uses DBMS in backend systems.

------------------------------------------------------------
📌 2. BANKING SYSTEMS 🏦
------------------------------------------------------------

🔹 Applications:
- Account management
- Transactions (deposit/withdraw)
- Loan records

🔹 Benefits:
✔ Secure transactions
✔ Accurate balance updates
✔ Fast data retrieval

❓ Interview Questions:
Q1. Why is DBMS used in banking systems?
👉 Because it ensures security, consistency, and fast transaction processing.

Q2. What DBMS feature is most important in banking?
👉 Transaction management (ACID properties).
